Bio

Birth

Logic was born January 22nd, 1990 and is currently 28 years old.

Family

Logic is biracial and has a black dad and a white mom. He raps about them in some of his songs.

Rise to Fame

Logic started out his career with Visionary Music Group and started to release mixtapes in around 2010. After awhile, his album Under Pressure, blew up on the Billboart Hot 100 and then his career sparked from there.
